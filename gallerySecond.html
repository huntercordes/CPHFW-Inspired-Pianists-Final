About

Sinead O'Dwyer offer intentionally designed luxury garments for women, femmes and gender non-conforming individuals of all shapes and sizes. Diverse bodies are at the core of their design process, from research and development to the final product. This passion and care for the human body in all its iterations and their radical approach to design, sampling and production is what makes them unique.
